WHAT WE DO

SOFTWARE
We develop open source, community software that helps geoscientists visualize and analysize their data.

EDUCATION
We help scientists learn how to make the most effective use of software and services for geoscience data analysis.

SERVICES
We help the scientific community communicate their findings to the public and peers through advanced visualization.

R&D
We conduct research aimed at improving our ability to make sense of the most complex and demanding data.
